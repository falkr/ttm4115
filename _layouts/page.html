About This Guide

I work as an associate professor at NTNU, where I am teaching engineering courses to students at different levels.

This site contains personal tips, instructions and reflections about how to make active learning work in practice. I mainly follow the approach of Team-Based Learning, so most of the entries in here are related to that. I accumulated them over the years working with active learning and TBL, either in my own courses or when helping others to get started with TBL. Whatever I recommend her is something I have either tried out in my own courses or observed others doing. My focus here is to be instructional and care mostly about implementation, everyday routines, and how to actually getting active learning done.
